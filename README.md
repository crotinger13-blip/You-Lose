# You-Lose
Privacy Policy — You Lose
Last updated: June 25, 2026
Overview
You Lose is a Rock Paper Scissors game developed for entertainment purposes. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.
Information We Collect
We collect no personal information whatsoever. You Lose does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other identifying information at any point.
Local Storage
The app stores your loss count and game progress locally on your device using your browser or device's built-in local storage. This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, not shared with any third party, and not accessible to the developer or anyone else. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app cache.
Third Party Services
You Lose does not integrate with any third party analytics platforms, advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers. No SDKs, trackers, or third party libraries that collect data are included in this app.
Children's Privacy
You Lose does not collect data from any users including children. The app is suitable for general audiences. Because no personal information is collected, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.
Advertising
This app contains no advertisements.
Data Security
Because we collect no data, there is no user data to secure, breach, or mishandle.
Changes to This Policy
If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
